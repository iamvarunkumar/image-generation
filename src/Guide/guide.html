How to Use?

1. Click on the Generate Button on the Homepage

Visit the homepage of the website and click on the "Generate" button to proceed.

2. Enter Prompt of the Image You Want to Generate

In the provided input field, enter the prompt or description of the image you want to generate. Be as specific and detailed as possible to get accurate results.

3. Choose How Many Images You Want to Generate

Select the desired quantity of images you want to generate based on your needs. Keep in mind that generating more images may require additional processing time.

4. Save to Library or Save to Device

Once the images are generated, you may have two options for saving them. You can choose to save the images directly to your personal image library on the website or download them to your device.

5. Not Satisfied? Click Regenerate

If you are not satisfied with the generated images, you may have an option to click on the "Regenerate" button to generate new images based on the same prompt.
